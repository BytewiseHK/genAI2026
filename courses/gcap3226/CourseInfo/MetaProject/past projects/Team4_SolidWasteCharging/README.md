## Overview

This past project analyzes **solid waste charging schemes** in Hong Kong through data-driven policy analysis and government engagement. It corresponds to **Team 4: Solid Waste Charging** from GCAP 3226 Fall 2025. Reference Spring 2026 topic: **solid-waste-charging** (reference past project only, not a group topic).

---

## Core research question

**How does the Hong Kong Environmental Protection Department use data-driven approaches in municipal solid waste charging policy design and implementation?**

Focus areas: government decision-making, public perception, behavioral impacts, effectiveness evaluation, data governance and transparency.

---

## What the team did

- **Government enquiries (Code on Access to Information):** EPD data on policy decisions, compliance, effectiveness.
- **Survey data:** GCAP3226_week2.csv — 99 validated responses; 18 Hong Kong districts; policy support, perceived fairness, behavioral indicators.
- **Published letters:** SCMP letters on university waste disclosure; residential recycling (Tai Po case study).
- **Mathematical models:** Regression (policy impact, compliance factors); simulation (scenario testing).
- **Report structure:** Government decision-making analysis → public perception → models → policy recommendations.
